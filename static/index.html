How it works
01
Discover
Scan domains, IP ranges, or cloud accounts. Find every SSH server, TLS endpoint, and cryptographic asset automatically.
02
Classify
Every algorithm is classified by quantum vulnerability. RSA, ECDSA, DH — flagged. Hybrid PQC, ML-KEM — noted. Pure PQC — green.
03
Inventory
Build a CBOM (Cryptography Bill of Materials) in CycloneDX 1.6 format. Tag assets with owner, environment, and upgrade status.
04
Report
Generate a consulting PDF with executive summary, critical findings, and a phased remediation roadmap tied to NIST PQC standards.
